The two are intimately linked. And history is our collective memory. If our collective memory is taken from us - is rewritten - we lose the ability to sustain our true selves.” ~Haruki Murakam MOCA Spike 150 provided me with a crash course on the first wave of Chinese in America. With this knowledge came a realization and a dose of responsibility that I now stand at an important crossroads in history. These Chinese laborers arrived in America and enabled the nation to realize its dream of connecting the East and the West. Yet our country’s history deprived them of their collective memory.
