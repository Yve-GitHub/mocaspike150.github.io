Let’s recreate this Chinatown mini marathon back in 1977!

The country just had its bicentennial. Long summers. Black outs. Roller skates. Platform shoes. Charlie's Angels. Star Wars. Chinatown gangs. Who knew running was a thing in 70's Chinatown!(/p)

https://www.mocaspike150.org/stories/2019-04-02-new-york-chinatown-mini-marathon

Join Us to Celebrate the Launch of MOCA Spike 150

It will be a MOCA Open House Day!

WELCOME! Runners, bring your friends and family to join the MOCA Spike 150 Launch Event and Chinatown Mini Marathon. Program includes:

• The Chinatown Mini Marathon, a 10k group run that starts and ends at MOCA.

• A self or guided museum tour from 10:00-11am, followed by a MOCA Spike 150 Launch Event with NYC City Council Speaker Corey Johnson, City Councilmember Margaret Chin and Manhattan Borough President Gale Brewer who will be present to represent a broad range of leadership and support from the City to MOCA and the MOCA Spike 150 initiative.

• Refreshment and breakfast will be served before and after run.

Date Time: 4/13/2019 8:30 am
Rain or shine, the run is on!
Location: Museum of Chinese in America (MOCA)
215 Centre Street New York, NY 10013
* Bag check service & refreshment provided on MOCA

Route Overview: Our run begins at MOCA at 9am, continues down Centre street, crosses the iconic Brooklyn Bridge, through Dumbo for a magnificent view of the Manhattan skyline, crosses the Williamsburg bridge and returns to MOCA.
