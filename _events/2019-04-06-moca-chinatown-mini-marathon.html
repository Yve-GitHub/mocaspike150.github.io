Location (Start and Finish): Museum of Chinese in America (MOCA)

* Bag check service & refreshment provided on MOCA

Join the Event

Route Overview: Our run begins at MOCA at 9am, continues down Centre street, crosses the iconic Brooklyn Bridge, through Dumbo for a magnificent view of the Manhattan skyline, crosses the Williamsburg bridge and returns to MOCA.

Let’s recreate this Chinatown mini marathon back in 1977!

https://www.mocaspike150.org/stories/2019-04-02-new-york-chinatown-mini-marathon

It will be a MOCA Open House Day!

Welcome runners bring your friends and family to join us. Refreshment and breakfast will be served before and after run. Self or guided museum tour from 10:00-11am, followed by a MOCA Spike 150 launch event with NYC City Council Speaker Corey Johnson, City Councilmember Margaret Chin, and Manhattan Borough President Gale Brewer to show City’s support to MOCA and MOCA Spike 150 initiative. Please come and join us!

MOCA会提供早餐与存包服务,当天博物馆将免费队跑者及朋友和家属开放参观. 11am-12pm将有MOCA Spike 150启动会,邀请媒体与New York City Council member Corey Johnson, Margaret Chin及Manhattan Borough President Gale Brewer到场给予致词, 表达紐約市对MOCA及MOCA Spike 150的支持. 欢迎一同加入同庆！
