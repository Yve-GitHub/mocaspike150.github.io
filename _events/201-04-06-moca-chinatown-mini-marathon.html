--- layout: post date: 2019-04-06 00:00:00 title: MOCA Chinatown Mini Marathon card-image: https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/46349226/55677549-f8ec3580-58b7-11e9-92bf-be471ea68051.JPG post-image: https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/46349226/55677559-381a8680-58b8-11e9-9b4c-5b5a787bd65a.jpg --- Time: Check in time: 8:00-9:00am (start running at 9am) Start/Finish Location: Museum in Chinese in America 215 Centre Street New York, NY 10013 * Bag check service & refreshment provided on MOCA Runners will leave MOCA at 9am run down Centre street, go across the iconic Brooklyn Bridge and run through Dumbo to enjoy the Manhattan skyline view from Brooklyn side. Take the Williamsburg bridge back and back to MOCA. Let's recreate this Chinatown mini marathon back in 1977! https://www.mocaspike150.org/stories/2019-04-02-new-york-chinatown-mini-marathon It will be a MOCA open house day! Welcome runners bring your friends and family to join us. Refreshment and breakfast will be served before and after run. Museum will provide a guided tour from 10:30-11am and followed by a MOCA Spike press launch event with guest speakers such as New York City Council member Corey Johnson and Margaret Chin to show City's support to MOCA and MOCA Spike 150 initiative. Please come and join us! MOCA会提供早餐与存包服务,当天博物馆将免费队跑者及朋友和家属开放参观. 11am-12pm将有MOCA Spike 150启动会,邀请媒体与New York City Council member Corey Johnson及Margaret Chin到场给予致词, 表达紐約市对MOCA及MOCA Spike 150的支持. 欢迎一同加入同庆！
